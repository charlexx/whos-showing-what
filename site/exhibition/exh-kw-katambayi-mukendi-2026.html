Jean Katambayi Mukendi: RATIO

Current  ·  21 February 2026 – 10 May 2026

Venue

KW Institute for Contemporary Art (institution)

Berlin, Germany

Artists

  • Jean Katambayi Mukendi Democratic Republic of the Congo

First solo exhibition in Germany by Congolese artist. Works created during six-week residency at KW using locally sourced recycled materials from Berlin. Addresses global structural inequities in resource extraction and power distribution. Central sculpture mukendi kabongo Air hybrid Wings. Co-produced with M HKA Antwerp.
