Paris Noir: Artistic Circulations and Anti-Colonial Resistance, 1950-2000

Past  ·  19 March 2025 – 30 June 2025

Venue

Centre Pompidou (museum)

Paris, France

Landmark final exhibition before Pompidou's 5-year closure. 350+ works by 150 Black artists from Africa, Americas and Caribbean who lived and worked in Paris 1950-2000. Retraces Negritude movement and Presence Africaine.
