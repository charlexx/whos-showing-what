Venice Biennale 2026: In Minor Keys

Upcoming  ·  9 May 2026 – 22 November 2026

Venue

Giardini & Arsenale (biennial-venue)

The 61st International Art Exhibition curated by the late Koyo Kouoh (Cameroon/Switzerland), the first African woman to lead the Venice Biennale. Features 111 artists and 12 African national pavilions. Title drawn from jazz and the idea of quieter, more attentive registers.
